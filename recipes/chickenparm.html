Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken Parm is by far one of my favorite meals! Here is a delcious recipe you can easily try at home! Get yourself some basic supplies from the grocery store and cook a restaurant quality meal at home. There are a few simple directions to follow and, before you know it, will have a new favorite dish to make. Best of luck!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Pasta

Instructions

  1. Bread chicken and cover with cheese
  2. Place chicken in a pan inside the oven, preheated to 350 degress
  3. Let cook until desired internal temp
  4. Cook pasta and heat up sauce
  5. Once chicken and pasta done cooking, lay a bed of pasta and cover with chicken and sauce
  6. Enjoy!
